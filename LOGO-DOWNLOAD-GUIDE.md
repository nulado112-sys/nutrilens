# How to Download and Add Restaurant Logos

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Download Logos from Instagram

For each restaurant, follow these steps:

#### **Method 1: Screenshot the Profile Picture (Easiest)**

1. Open Instagram on your phone
2. Go to the restaurant's profile page
3. Tap on the profile picture to view it full screen
4. Take a screenshot
5. Crop the image to just the logo (square format)
6. Save to your phone

#### **Method 2: Use Instagram Story Downloader (Better Quality)**

1. Go to a website like `insta-downloader.net` or `storysaver.net`
2. Copy the Instagram profile URL
3. Paste it into the downloader
4. Download the profile picture

### 2. Transfer Logos to Your Computer

**From iPhone:**
- AirDrop to your Mac
- Or email to yourself
- Or use iCloud Photos

**From Android:**
- Use Google Photos
- Or USB cable transfer
- Or email to yourself

### 5. Image Requirements

- **Format**: PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Size**: At least 300x300 pixels (square)
- **Background**: Transparent PNG works best, but white background is fine

### 7. Using VS Code to Add Images

1. Open VS Code
2. In the left sidebar, navigate to the `assets/restaurants/` folder
3. Drag and drop the logo files from your desktop into the correct restaurant folder
4. Make sure the file is named exactly `logo.png` (lowercase)

### 8. Test Your Website

After adding all logos:
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Check that all restaurant logos appear correctly
3. If a logo doesn't show, check:
   - Is the file named exactly `logo.png`?
   - Is it in the correct folder?
   - Try refreshing the page (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
